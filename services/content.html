FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We create blog posts (500-3000+ words), Twitter/X threads, LinkedIn articles and posts, Instagram captions, Facebook posts, email sequences (welcome, nurture, sales), newsletter editions, ad copy (Google, Meta, LinkedIn), product descriptions, press releases, landing page copy, and more. If it involves words, we can create it. Custom formats are available on Business and Enterprise plans.
During onboarding, you share examples of content you like, your brand guidelines, and your target audience description. Our AI analyzes your existing content (website, social media, emails) to extract tone, vocabulary patterns, sentence structure, and personality traits. We create an internal style guide that ensures every piece we create sounds authentically like your brand. On Business and Enterprise plans, we refine this with each batch.
Every blog post and article includes keyword research, optimized title tags and meta descriptions, proper heading hierarchy (H1-H3), internal linking suggestions, image alt text recommendations, and readability optimization. Business and Enterprise plans include competitor analysis, keyword gap identification, and content cluster strategy. We target keywords with realistic ranking potential based on your domain authority.
Starter plans include 1 revision per piece, Business plans include 3 revisions per piece, and Enterprise plans include unlimited revisions. A revision covers changes to tone, structure, length, keywords, or specific sections. We aim to get it right on the first draft by deeply understanding your brand voice upfront, but revisions ensure you are 100% satisfied with every piece before publishing.
Our standard plans support English and German content creation. Enterprise plans offer multi-language support including Spanish, French, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, and more. We create native-quality content, not translations. Each language version is crafted by AI models specifically fine-tuned for that language and reviewed for cultural nuance. Need a language not listed? Contact us and we will find a solution.
