Pro tips

Add more patterns: The more patterns you add, the smarter your bot gets. Use regex like /ship|deliver|track/i to match multiple keywords.

E-commerce example: Add patterns for shipping, returns, sizing, availability.
SaaS example: Add patterns for pricing tiers, integrations, API, free trial.
Portfolio example: Add patterns for services, availability, rates, process.

GDPR: NexBot processes everything in the browser. Zero data is sent anywhere. You can honestly state "no data collection" in your privacy policy for the chat widget.
