OG Tag Best Practices

Use Unique Titles

Each page should have a unique og:title. Avoid duplicating your homepage title across all pages. Keep titles under 60 characters for best display.

High-Quality Images

Use images at least 1200x630px. JPEG or PNG format works best. Avoid text-heavy images as they may be cropped differently per platform.

Test Before Publishing

Use Facebook's Sharing Debugger and Twitter's Card Validator to confirm your tags are parsed correctly after deploying changes.

Include All Required Tags

At minimum, include og:title, og:description, og:image, and og:url. Missing any of these may cause platforms to generate poor previews.
