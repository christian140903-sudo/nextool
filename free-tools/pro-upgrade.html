Is it really a lifetime license?
Yes. You pay $49 once and get access to the extension forever, including all future updates and new tools we add. There are no recurring charges, no annual renewals, and no hidden fees. As long as Chrome exists, the extension works.
Do I need an API key?
For the AI-powered features, you bring your own API key (BYOK). This means you pay AI providers directly at their wholesale rates instead of a marked-up subscription. We support OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and more. Non-AI tools work without any key.
What browsers are supported?
NexTool Pro works on all Chromium-based browsers: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, Arc, Vivaldi, and Opera. Firefox support is on our roadmap. The extension installs in seconds from the Chrome Web Store.

We offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If the extension isn't working for you, send us a message and we'll refund your payment within 48 hours. We want you to be confident in your purchase.
