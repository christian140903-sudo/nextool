Getting the most out of NEXUS

FORGE: Start with "forge_analyze" to get template recommendations for any task.
CORTEX: Use "cortex_learn" after solving problems. The AI gets smarter over time.
SWARM: Use "swarm_decompose" for complex multi-step tasks. It creates parallel plans.
SENTINEL: Schedule "sentinel_check" daily to monitor your projects automatically.
HACKATHON: Use "hack_analyze" at the start of any project for instant strategy.
