Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

NexTool ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Christian Jun Bucher, based in Austria. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Austrian data protection laws.

1. What Data We Collect

When you use NexTool for free:

  • We do not collect personal data.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking scripts.
  • Your expertise packs are generated and stored entirely in your browser. We never see or store your prompts.

When you purchase a paid plan:

  • Payment data is processed by PayPal. We never see or store your credit card or bank details.
  • PayPal may share your name and email address with us for the purpose of fulfilling your subscription.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain our service.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To communicate with you about your account (only if you contact us).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)) — Processing payment data to fulfill your subscription.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — Improving our service and preventing fraud.

4. Data Retention

Payment records are retained for 7 years as required by Austrian tax law (BAO §132). All other data is deleted when no longer needed for its original purpose.

5. Third-Party Services

  • PayPal (PayPal (Europe) S.à r.l.) — Payment processing. PayPal Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.) — Website hosting. No personal data is transmitted. GitHub Privacy Statement

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As an EU/EEA resident, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (Art. 15)
  • Rectify inaccurate data (Art. 16)
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten") (Art. 17)
  • Restrict processing (Art. 18)
  • Data portability (Art. 20)
  • Object to processing (Art. 21)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at christian140903@gmail.com.

7. Data Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Payment processing is handled entirely by PayPal's PCI DSS-certified infrastructure. We do not store sensitive data on our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

NexTool is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
