Our Story

A Tradition of Flavor

For over three decades, Bella's Kitchen has been a cornerstone of Viennese dining. Founded by Nonna Isabella in 1987 with nothing more than her mother's recipes and an unwavering passion for real food, our kitchen still honors the same principles today.

Every dish begins with ingredients sourced from trusted local farms and imported directly from Italy. Our pasta is made fresh each morning. Our sauces simmer for hours. There are no shortcuts -- only love, patience, and respect for the craft.

-- Isabella & Family
